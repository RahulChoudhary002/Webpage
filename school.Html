AIS School

AIS [American International School] in Jaipur is a prominent educational institution affiliated with the CBSE [Central Board of Secondary Education]. Established with a vision to provide a holistic education, AIS emphasizes both academic excellence and character development. The school boasts a modern campus equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including well-equipped classrooms, science and computer labs, libraries, and sports facilities. AIS focuses on a balanced curriculum that integrates academics with co-curricular activities, fostering creativity and critical thinking among students. The school encourages participation in various events, including debates, art competitions, and sports, promoting teamwork and leadership skills. Dedicated faculty members at AIS are committed to nurturing each student's potential, offering personalized attention to ensure a conducive learning environment. The school also emphasizes the importance of values and ethics, preparing students to become responsible global citizens. Extracurricular programs at AIS enhance students' overall development, providing opportunities for personal growth and social awareness. With a strong emphasis on innovation and technology, AIS equips students with the skills necessary for the future. Overall, Amity International School Jaipur stands out as a leading educational institution, dedicated to shaping well-rounded individuals ready to face the challenges of tomorrow.
